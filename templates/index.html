📰 How it decides to buy

Every 30 min (one "cycle") the bot reads the latest crypto news headlines for each coin on your watchlist.

Claude AI scores the news sentiment 1–10. If the score meets your risk threshold and the technical indicators (RSI, MACD) are not flashing danger, the bot buys.

Strategy: buy when news is strongly positive and indicators confirm — a variant of "buy the news, sell the rally."

💰 How it decides to sell

The bot automatically sells a position when one of these triggers is hit:

  • Profit Target — price rose to your take-profit % (locks in gains)
  • Stop Loss — price fell to your stop-loss % (limits losses)
  • RSI Overbought — RSI above 80 (technically overextended)
  • Negative Sentiment — news turns very bearish mid-hold
🧠 How it learns

Every 5 cycles, the AI reviews the last 25 closed trades: what score led to wins, what led to losses, time-of-day patterns, etc.

It then adjusts the buy threshold and scoring weights automatically — within safe bounds so it can't over-correct itself.

You can also trigger a manual review anytime in the "AI Strategy Learner" section below.
